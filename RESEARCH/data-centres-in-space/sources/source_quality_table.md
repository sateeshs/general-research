# Source Quality Ratings

**Rating Scale:**
- **A**: Peer-reviewed journals, systematic reviews, government agency official reports
- **B**: Established industry analysis, reputable news with named sources, space agency publications
- **C**: Expert opinion, company press releases, industry blogs with verifiable claims
- **D**: Preprints, preliminary research, technology blogs
- **E**: Anecdotal, theoretical, speculative, unverified
